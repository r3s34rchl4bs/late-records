Guide

Shipping

Orders are prepared and shipped within 1–2 business days. Once dispatched, a tracking number will be provided. Each record is carefully packed in sturdy mailers to ensure safe delivery.

Payments

Payments can be made through Philippine e-wallets and local banks. We currently accept GCash, Maya, GoTyme, and local bank transfers when ordering from our online shop.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept return requests within 7 days of receiving your order.

If your order arrives incorrect, contact us within 3 days of delivery with clear photos or an unboxing video. Once confirmed, we will arrange a replacement or refund.

If your order arrives damaged due to shipping, contact us within 3 days of delivery with photos showing the damage and packaging. We will assist in filing a claim with the courier.

Due to the nature of vinyl records, minor sleeve wear or cosmetic imperfections that do not affect playback may not qualify for a return.

To report an issue, email info.late.records@gmail.com with your order number and details.
