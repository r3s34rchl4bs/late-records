    Shipping

    Orders are prepared and shipped within 1–2 business days. Once dispatched, a tracking number will be provided. Each record is carefully packed in sturdy mailers.

    Shipping Rates

    ₱250 for 1–2 records. ₱350 for 3–5 records. ₱450 for 6 or more.

    Payments

    We accept GCash, Maya, GoTyme, and local bank transfers. A QR code will appear on the confirmation page after placing your order.

    Returns & Exchanges

    Return requests accepted within 7 days of receiving your order.

    If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 3 days with photos or an unboxing video. Once confirmed, we'll arrange a replacement or refund.
